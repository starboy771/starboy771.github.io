Images

Images serve as visual representations that showcase a company's products and services Images can be photos, drawings, charts, diagrams and other graphics to contribute to the design and asthetic of the webpage.

Here are the several formats that can be use for appropriate design

  • GIF - Graphics Interchange Format and is pronounced as “jiff”. GIF is the oldest web file format and supports transparency and frame animation
  • PNG - Portable Network Graphic and is pronounce as “ping”. PNG utilizes lossless compression and can accommodate 8-bit color, 16-bit grayscale, and 24-bit true-color images.
  • JPG - Joint Photographic Experts Group and is pronounced as “jay-peg”. JPG is ideal for high-detail images like digital photos for its support of 16.7 million colors in a 24-bit format, making it perfect for intricate details and color compositions
  • SVG - Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG) is a file format that employs markup language to generate two-dimensional graphics, images, and animations, offering scalability without loss of quality.
