Campus Tours

Considering our school for your educational endeavors? Start with a campus tour! Let us show you our beautiful campus along with everything you need to know to get started as a new student.

Student Socials

New here? Come mix and mingle with fellow students every Monday and Friday at 4pm in the Activities Center. Meet current students who gave share helpful tips. Appetizers and soft drinks provided.

Enjoy live music with DJ Flip

Resume Workshop

Need a resume but not sure where to start? Come to our free resume workshop to learn how to put together a resume to start your new career. The workshop is held every Tuesday at 3pm and is open to all students.

Students are welcome to bring a friend or relative to any of these events. Suggestions for other events are also welcome.
