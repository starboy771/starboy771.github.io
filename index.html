    What are Pokemon?

    Pokemon are creatures that trainers can catch, train, and battle with.

    The dream of most Pokemon Trainers is to become the greatest Pokemon Master.

    Pokemon can be used for both good or evil. It all depends on their trainers.

    Some people could veiw Pokemon as tools. Some see them as pets. And some see them as family.

    Pokemon are capable of feeling human emmosions and can understand the feelings of their trainers.

    Background on Pokemon

    The word Pokemon is short for "Pocket Monster."

    Pokemon exssist in video games, comics, tv shows, and all sorts of media.

    The games are around battling other trainers and beating the leaders of the Pokemon Gyms to get stronger and enter the Pokemon Leage.

    The Tv shows follow the adventure of a Pokemon trainer as they explore the many reigens in the world of Pokemon to become the strongest Pokemon master of all time.

    The comics are useally based on the games and most are mangas.

    The Importent Points of Pokemon

    A Pokemon trainer starts their Pokeon journey by getting their first Pokemon from the Pokemon labs in their towns.

    Once obtaining their first Pokemon, trainers will use their Pokemon to battle wild Pokemon to weaken them enough to capture them.

    Once a Pokemon is captured, it'll be bound to you until you willingly let it go. A trainer can only carry six Pokemon at a time. Any extra Pokemon captured are placed in a PC that you can check at in Pokemon centers.

    Other trainers will battle you for fun and it's a good why to strenghthen your Pokemon.

    Pokemon Centers are used as a place to heal your Pokemon if it's badly damaged.
